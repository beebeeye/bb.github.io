Why Pain?

Challenges lie in the following aspects:
(1) At the moment, owner may change their decisions and requirements, even the function of the whole building, which will cause enormous work to the architects.

(2) During the construction, some parts of the design may be found infeasible. Architects have to modify the design accordingly.

(3) Architects sometimes have to struggle with a tough construction team to ensure the building constructed as designed.
