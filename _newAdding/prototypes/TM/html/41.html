Description

Create the site plan by drawing the footprint of the design by hand on the site survey previously purchased from the Urban Planning Department. Submit the site plan to the Urban Planning Department for approval. Because the purchased site survey is a paper copy, the architects needs to draw the footprint and important coordinates manually in red or blue lines. The Urban Planning Department requires three copies of the site plan.

Why Pain?

It is a pain because of the manual work on the hard copy site survey.
