Description

Develop the program based on the owner's requirements.

>Design codes: The program covers "economic requirements" such as architecture density, floor area ratio (=total floor area of buildings on a site divided by total site area), area, number of levels, functions, etc.



Why Pain?

The pain is due to the lack of standards for owners' requirement documents, and the owners' lack of architecture professional background. Not all owners have a clear idea of what they want and what's possible. Requirements from owner’s lack of architecture background are sometimes lack of details, unreasonable, or impossible. It takes a lot of communication to clarify requirements and develop the program.

Workarounds

The only support from technology that the participants could think of, to resolve the pain is a document template for program.
