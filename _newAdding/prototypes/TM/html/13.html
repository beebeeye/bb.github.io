Description

In this step, the surrounding environment of the site is taken into account in preparation for the architecture design. The focus of a site analysis could include the surrounding business, public transportation, economic factors, culture and residence, functional surroundings, etc. No report is needed. But the site analysis results are usually used as the design rationale in the Design Specification.
