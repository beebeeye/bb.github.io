Description

Create formal design documents. If it is a bidding project, the design firm cannot appear on the cover page. If it is a commission project, the design firm, its qualification and stamps have to be on the cover page. Required stamps include those of Class 1 Registered Architects (PRC) for the project and those presenting the qualifications of the design firm. Design firm should prepare 3 copies of the documents for the Urban Planning Department and 1 to 2 copies for their own archive.

It will be a pain point if the Urban Planning Department requires modification in the submission.
