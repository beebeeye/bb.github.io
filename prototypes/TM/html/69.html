Description

Peer review is required by law. The reviewer, called "Architecture Design Inspection Firm", is chosen by the owner and sometimes referred by the design firm.

> Architecture design inspection firms:
Architecture design inspection firms are not chosen by the government, but they should have specific qualifications and be licensed.

> Submittals for peer review:
The submittals include all the documents of all disciplines. Architecture: plans, elevations, sections, detailings; Structure: Rebar details、Beam and slab details、foundation details、structural detailing. 3. MEP: plans and detailing.
