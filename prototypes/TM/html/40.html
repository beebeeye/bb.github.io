Description

The following government departments are usually included in the design review board: the Urban Planning Department, the Fire Department, the Public Health Department, the Traffic Department, etc. Each department inspects the design strictly based on codes in their domain. They have specific requirements to the format of the submission. A submission is typically 2D plans, and sometimes descriptions in text.
